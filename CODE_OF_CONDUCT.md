# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in CrumbContext a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, colour, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful, specific, and evidence-driven.
- Critique ideas and implementations, not people.
- Use synthetic or redacted fixtures when discussing sensitive context.
- Acknowledge uncertainty and benchmark limitations.
- Help newcomers reproduce problems before judging conclusions.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, doxxing, deliberate disclosure of private prompts or credentials, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

Report conduct concerns privately to XIO AI Solutions. Maintainers will review reports in good faith and protect reporter privacy where possible.

This code is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
